I'm David Lapsley — Founder & CEO of ShipKodeAI, where we're building AI-accelerated software development lifecycle tooling.

I've spent my career at the intersection of distributed systems, infrastructure at scale, and engineering leadership. Before ShipKodeAI, I held senior engineering and leadership roles across startups and enterprises, shipping systems that served millions of users.

## What I Write About

This blog covers the topics I work on daily:

- **AI Infrastructure** — Production AI systems, inference at scale, and the cloud-native AI stack.
- **Agentic SDLC** — How AI agents are reshaping the software development lifecycle, from spec to deployment.
- **Distributed Systems** — Architecture patterns, trade-offs, and lessons learned from building at scale.
- **Engineering Leadership** — Building high-performance teams, product-engineering alignment, and the human side of shipping software.
